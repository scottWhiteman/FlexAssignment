About Me

When I used to play plenty of video games and use computer applications back then, I wanted to learn more about making them. I found that I enjoyed programming and analyzing why programs act the way they do.

I also like understanding designs, what looks appealing to the eye and what helps people see ergonomically. While I am also interested in learning to illustrate and animate, I find that Web Development lets me bridge both coding and design together and enjoy the process.

As I continue learning as a Web Developer, I am still interested in learning to animate and once in a while I play video games.

Even though I tend to spend more time to myself and my investments, I also have fun working with other people and teams: it's always more lively with other people, so don't be afraid to ping me with a message if you need a developer!
